# Guide

> [!NOTE]
> This guide assumes you're using the [Prism Launcher](https://prismlauncher.org/) to play minecraft.
>
> Just download it, double click the file to start installation, go through the steps, log in to your minecraft account, and that's it

## Install mods for the first time

1. Download `Ferncraft.mrpack` from the latest [release](https://github.com/unxok/ferncraft-mods/releases)
2. In Prism Launcher, right click in the empty space and choose **Create instance**.
3. In the popup, choose the **Import** option on the left side.
4. Click **Browse** and choose the `Ferncraft.mrpack` you just downloaded then press **OK** to complete the import.
5. Now you'll see an instance called **Ferncraft**.
   - Double click this to launch minecraft.
   - Right click and press **Edit** to do things like add resource packs and shader packs.

## Update mod list for existing instance

Simply follow the steps above, and choose **Update existing instance** when it pops up.
